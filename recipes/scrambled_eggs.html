Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are the staple of most breakfasts around the world. They're simple, nutritious, and delicious. Here's how to quickly make them!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Sea Salt
  • Ground Pepper
  • Butter (1-2 Tablespoons)

Directions

  1. Take 2 eggs and crack them into a bowl.
  2. Add a couple shakes of salt and pepper to the bowl.
  3. Cut butter into small cubes and add to bowl.
  4. Beat/Whisk eggs until they appear all yellow.
  5. Put pan on medium-high heat and add the eggs.
  6. Cook until done, then add any additional salt and pepper to taste.
