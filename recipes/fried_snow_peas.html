Stir-Fried Snow Peas

Description

Chinese stir-fry is delicious. These stir-fried snow peas will be delicious too!

Ingredients

  • Snow peas
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vegetable Oil

Directions

  1. (Optional) Peel off the edges of the snow peas to make them easier to chew.
  2. Rinse your snow peas.
  3. Put a pan on medium-high heat and add oil. Wait for a couple minutes before adding peas.
  4. After adding the peas, liberally add soy sauce to pan.
  5. Cook until peas turn dark green.
