Oven Roasted Potatoes

Description

Here we have some delicious oven roasted potatoes. They take some time to cook, but they're worth it!

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Ground Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Cut the potatoes into small, bite-sized cubes.
  2. Preheat your oven to 400°F (205°C).
  3. Boil potatoes in heavily salted water until they start to fall apart.
  4. Place potatoes on a greased baking sheet and coat with olive oil, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  5. Put pan on medium-high heat and add the eggs.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes, turn the potatoes, and then bake for another 30 minutes until a crust forms.
